About Me

Ben Mulholland is a Full Stack Web Developer, Designer, and Composer based in Philadelphia, PA. As a former educator who taught block-based coding and music software to K-12th grade students for over a decade, he is known for a fluvial-calm personality with strength in collaboration in creativity, and diversity, embracing teams.

A graduate in Full Stack Web Development from the University of Pennsylvania LPS' bootcamp program, Ben applies his experience and skills to solving problems and meaningfully contributing to his community. Music education and technology are two of his biggest passions.
